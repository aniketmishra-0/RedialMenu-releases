RedialMenu lives in your menu bar. Press a hotkey and a circular wheel of your
apps appears right under your cursor. Glide to the app you want and let go — it
switches instantly. A faster, more visual alternative to `⌘ + Tab`.

## Install

1. Download `RedialMenu.dmg` from the button above.
2. Open it and drag **RedialMenu** into your **Applications** folder.
3. Launch it from Applications — it appears as a menu-bar icon.

> macOS may warn that the app is from an unidentified developer. Right-click the
> app → **Open** to bypass this on first launch. Grant **Accessibility**
> permission when prompted so the global hotkey and app switching work.

## Requirements

- macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
